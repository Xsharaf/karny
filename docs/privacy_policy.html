Privacy Policy for Karny

Effective date: June 9, 2025

This privacy policy describes how the mobile application Karny (package: com.silverfang.karny) handles user information.

1. Data Collection

Karny does not directly collect personal data. However, it uses third-party services that may collect limited, non-personal data for advertising and performance analytics purposes.

2. Local Data Storage

All user-generated data, such as grocery lists and item details, is stored locally on the user’s device and is not shared or transmitted elsewhere.

3. Advertising with Google AdMob

Karny uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and use information such as:

  • Device identifiers
  • Approximate location (e.g. for region-specific ads)
  • App usage and interaction data

This data helps deliver relevant and non-intrusive ads and is handled according to Google’s privacy policies.

For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Encryption

All data exchanged with external services (such as AdMob) is encrypted in transit using HTTPS.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is not specifically directed toward children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent and believe your child has submitted data, you may contact us to request deletion.
